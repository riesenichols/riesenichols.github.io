Introduction

Eye Scream

Eye Scream is the go-to local ice cream shop in San Luis Obispo. Customers are impressed by Eye Scream's trendy aesthetic and abundance of fun flavors offered. Eye Scream used only the highest quality of ingredients to create our delicious ice creams.

Target audience

We cater to a wide range of an audience. From familes, to students, to anywhere in between. Our shop is in the heart of town so we get an audience of whoever loves this sweet treat! Our goal is to have the most exciting flavors with the highest quality ingredients. We want to make everyone's wildest ice cream dreams come true.
